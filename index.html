VaxEx is a mobile application that allows the users document their experiences or symptoms after they get vaccinated. Why VaxEx?

All over the world, people are blessed to have vaccines that can protect them from COVID-19. Vaccines helped boost our immune system in order to prevent the virus from spreading and with the way the world has been reformed by the pandemic, this brings us a step closer towards going back to the way things were.

However, even though these vaccines have gone through numerous of tests in labs, health workers especially the the Department of Health and the Food and Drug Administration cannot track what we call the ADR or Adverse Drug Reaction of these vaccines. ADR refers to to unwanted, uncomfortable, or dangerous effects that a drug may have and this app will help to gather the data needed to improve the researches against the virus.
